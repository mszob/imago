The board is built around a few, straightforward design features:

  • Keys are arranged in a simple grid layout rather than the more unintuitive staggered layout leftover from the typewriter era
  • All keys are within the reach of resting hands
  • Multiple layers allow neat an intuitive organizing of functions with limited keys — think of a shift key on steroids
  • Software and hardware are fully customizable

A thoughtful combined use of these features can lead to a typing experience in which keys and key-combinations seems to come effortlessly to your fingertips.
